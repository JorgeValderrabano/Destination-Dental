THE DESTINATION DENTAL DIFFERENCE

High-Quality Dental Care

We’re revolutionizing dental tourism at Destination Dental, akin to what Expedia has done for travel. We strictly mandate that all our partner clinics adhere to and often surpass the American dental care standards. Our role is akin to a dental concierge, ensuring you only receive care from top-tier clinics that we’ve thoroughly vetted and personally approved. All of our clinics are English speaking, and meet our rigorous standards for quality.

Affordable Dental Solutions

Thanks to our unique business model, we’re able to bring a high volume of patients to our approved dental clinics. This allows us to negotiate lower prices, offering you affordable dental solutions that would typically cost more if you approached the clinics directly. We’re transforming dental care to be more cost-effective without compromising on quality. Experience world-class dental treatments at a fraction of U.S. costs, allowing you to achieve your perfect smile without breaking the bank.

Comprehensive Support

With Destination Dental, you’re never alone. Our dedicated team of patient advocates and travel agents will assist you from start to finish, making sure your journey to a healthier smile is smooth and hassle-free. They’ll handle all your travel arrangements, and even be your personal transportation once you arrive in Cancun.

Personalized Service

Our commitment to you goes beyond dental care. Our patient advocates will accompany you to every appointment, ready to answer any questions and ensure your comfort. Consider them your personal dental concierges who are with you every step of the way, making your entire trip as easy and stress-free as possible. We believe that personalized service is key to a rewarding dental tourism experience.

how we work?

1. REACH OUT TO US

Share your relevant information about the treatment you want. If you have x-rays, pictures or a treatment plan, please send us those to help us better understand your dental situation. We'll guide you through the process of accessing high-quality, affordable dental care in Mexico.

2. Treatment Proposals

Our patient advocates will provide you with treatment proposals from the three dentists that best fit your dental needs. We will share their suggestions and treatment plans then explain the details, costs and help you compare them so that you fully understand your options.

3. SCHEDULE a videocall

We will schedule a time for you to have a video conference with the dentist that you feel best meets your dental needs. This will help you and the dentist or surgeon to understand your dental needs, the steps necessary to accomplish your treatment and make sure you all of your questions and concerns have been addressed.

4. ChoosE YOUR PLAN

We are here to assist you in making an informed decision that best suits your dental needs and financial situation. Once you have decided on a treatment plan and clinic, we will start planning your travel arrangements.

Make it affordable, get in touch with us to know our payment plans.
